!1 Car Park Operator can change the daily Parking Rate

!3 Story
As a Car park operator
I want to be able to set the cost of parking in my car park (instead of the current fixed price of £5 per day).
This improves expansion

!3 Rules
Standard all-day rate is £5
Off-peak (after 10am) is £3
Weekly (up to 7 days) is £20
Weekend daily rate (Sat/Sun) is £2
